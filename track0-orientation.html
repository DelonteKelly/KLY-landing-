Track 0 — Orientation & Program Entry

Welcome to the Kelly Legacy Institute Fiduciary Training Program.

Kelly Legacy Institute (“KLI”) is a private educational organization providing instruction in fiduciary literacy, governance principles, trust administration concepts, procedural analysis, and administrative recordkeeping practices.

This program is structured and sequential in nature. Participants are expected to complete lessons, assignments, and program requirements in the prescribed order.

Program Expectations

  • adherence to program instructions and participation guidelines
  • maintenance of personal study notes and educational records
  • respectful and professional conduct within the learning environment
  • compliance with applicable program policies and Terms of Service

This program is intended for educational purposes only and is not a substitute for legal, tax, financial, or professional advice.

Participation in this program does not: create an attorney-client relationship, create a fiduciary relationship, confer trustee authority or fiduciary status, grant governmental authority or legal standing, or authorize participants to act on behalf of the Institute or any third party.

Track 0 Orientation Tasks

Task 1 – Instruction Replication Exercise

“Procedure precedes remedy.
Record precedes recognition.
Capacity determines consequence.
Structure determines outcome.”

Reproduce the above statement exactly in your submission.

Task 2 – Structured Submission Exercise

Prepare and submit a properly organized orientation submission using the format:

Name:
Date:
Task Completed:
Statement of Completion:

Task 3 – Procedural Sequencing Exercise

Correctly organize the following educational process sequence:

Instruction    Action    Record    Verification

Place them in correct order (e.g., 1. Instruction, 2. Action, 3. Record, 4. Verification).

Submission & Verification

All Track 0 orientation submissions must be posted within the designated program category: ENTRY & ORIENTATION (on Skool or designated platform).

Include the following statement: “I have completed the required Track 0 orientation tasks and submitted this entry in accordance with the applicable program instructions.”

After submitting, your orientation will be reviewed. Advancement to Track 1 requires successful completion.

KLY Legacy Course

This course is only available to wallets holding 500+ KLY tokens.

Module 1: Foundation

Overview

This module lays the groundwork for your journey into Web3 entrepreneurship by teaching how to structure a legal business entity. You’ll learn how to use LLCs, trusts, and strategic ownership planning to protect your assets and create a legacy that lasts generations.

Objectives

  • Register a legal entity (LLC) in your state.
  • Set up an Operating Agreement.
  • Understand the role of trusts in asset protection.
  • Strategically assign ownership.
  • Open business bank accounts and DUNS profile.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Form an LLC.
  2. Draft an Operating Agreement.
  3. Obtain an EIN from the IRS.
  4. Establish a Family Trust (optional but powerful).
  5. Open a Business Bank Account.
  6. Register for a DUNS number.

Tools & Resources

  • NJ Business Portal
  • IRS EIN Assistant
  • Rocket Lawyer for Operating Agreements

Real-World Example

Delonte creates "Kelly Legacy Estate LLC", assigning ownership through a trust to secure generational wealth and future token activities.

Module 2: Tokenization

Overview

Learn to create your own token, structure its economics, and use the KLY Launchpad to launch purpose-driven blockchain projects.

Objectives

  • Understand tokens and their standards (ERC-20, ERC-721, ERC-1155).
  • Launch a token using KLY Launchpad.
  • Define supply, utility, and governance models.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Access KLY Launchpad.
  2. Define token details: Name, Symbol, Total Supply.
  3. Launch and verify your token on BNB Smart Chain.

Tools & Resources

  • KLY Launchpad
  • BNB Smart Chain
  • BscScan Token Verification

Real-World Example

Kenyetta launches "LegacyToken" for her educational platform and builds a reward ecosystem around it.

Module 4: Credit & Capital

Overview

Learn to build business credit under your LLC, access funding, and leverage your blockchain assets as proof of value and community trust.

Objectives

  • Establish business credit profiles.
  • Secure funding through fintech and blockchain-based reputation.
  • Separate personal and business financial profiles.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Set up Net-30 vendor accounts.
  2. Apply for business credit cards.
  3. Link blockchain assets as additional proof of business revenue.

Tools & Resources

  • Dun & Bradstreet (DUNS Number)
  • Divvy, Brex, Ramp (business credit cards)
  • BscScan dashboards

Real-World Example

Brielle secures over $15,000 in funding for her Web3 business using a hybrid of business credit and blockchain asset reporting.

Module 5: Ownership & Legacy

Overview

Plan the long-term protection and transfer of your assets using digital estate planning, smart contracts, and family trusts integrated with blockchain.

Objectives

  • Protect digital and business assets.
  • Assign blockchain assets in legal trusts.
  • Document and secure your digital estate plan.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Create a Living Trust.
  2. Secure wallets with hardware devices.
  3. Document and encrypt your digital legacy plan.
  4. Assign beneficiaries using multisig wallets or smart contracts.

Tools & Resources

  • Trust & Will (estate planning)
  • Ledger/Trezor hardware wallets
  • Gnosis Safe (multi-sig)

Real-World Example

Justice protects his family’s KLY assets by building a living trust tied to their multisig wallet for seamless inheritance without probate.

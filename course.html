KLY Legacy Course

Module 1: Foundation

Learn how to structure your business legally using LLCs, trusts, and strategic ownership setup. This module covers New Jersey requirements and how to set up long-term asset protection.

Module 2: Tokenization

Dive into Web3 by learning how to launch your own token using the KLY Launchpad. You'll learn how supply, symbol, treasury fees, and smart contracts work together.

Module 3: Web3 Business

Explore the difference between traditional business models and token-powered business systems. Learn how to use KLY to power courses, products, and reward systems.

Module 4: KLY Ecosystem

Understand staking, liquidity, token dashboards, and smart contract tools like Thirdweb. Learn to build reputation and utility around your token.

Module 5: Certification & Beyond

Complete the course to unlock your NFT certification. This module guides you through submission and how to use your certification to launch your Web3 project.

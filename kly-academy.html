Module 1: What is DeFi?

DeFi, or Decentralized Finance, refers to a financial system built on blockchain technology that operates without traditional banks or intermediaries.

Key Concepts

  • Smart Contracts
  • Permissionless
  • Transparency
  • Non-Custodial

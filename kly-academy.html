DeFi Basics

What is DeFi?

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) is a revolutionary financial system built on blockchain technology that eliminates intermediaries like banks and brokers. It provides open, permissionless access to financial services through smart contracts.

Key Insight

DeFi gives you complete control over your assets while providing transparency that traditional finance can't match. Your keys, your crypto.

Core Components

  • Blockchain: The immutable ledger that records all transactions
  • Smart Contracts: Self-executing code that powers DeFi applications
  • DAOs: Decentralized Autonomous Organizations that govern protocols
  • dApps: Decentralized applications that provide financial services

Why DeFi Matters

Traditional finance is plagued by slow transactions, high fees, and limited access. DeFi solves these problems by:

  • Operating 24/7 with no downtime
  • Reducing fees by eliminating middlemen
  • Providing global access to financial services
  • Offering transparency through on-chain data

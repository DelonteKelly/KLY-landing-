FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the utility of KLY token?

KLY token serves multiple purposes: access to educational content, governance voting rights, staking rewards, and as the primary currency within our ecosystem. Token holders can stake to earn passive income, vote on protocol changes, and unlock premium features.

How can I stake my KLY tokens?

Staking is available through our official staking portal. Connect your wallet, approve the staking contract, and deposit your KLY tokens. You'll start earning rewards immediately based on the current APY and your staked amount.

Is KLY token audited?

Yes, our smart contracts have been audited by reputable blockchain security firms. Audit reports are available on our documentation page for transparency.

What chains does KLY support?

Currently KLY operates on BNB Chain, with plans to expand to Ethereum, Polygon, and other EVM-compatible chains in 2025 through our cross-chain bridge.

How does the DAO governance work?

KLY holders can propose and vote on protocol changes. Each token represents one vote. Major decisions require a quorum of at least 10% of circulating supply participating, with a majority (51%) needed to pass proposals.
